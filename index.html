How it works

From health data to a practical daily plan

Fytvio is built around one simple question: based on your body and your goal, what should you do next?

01
Connect health data
Sync from Apple Health on iOS and Google Health Connect on Android. Fytvio reads supported metrics such as HRV, resting heart rate, sleep, steps, distance, calories, workouts, and body weight when available.
02
Create a goal plan
Turn a goal like running farther, improving consistency, adding yoga, or building strength into short daily tasks that can be completed inside the app.
03
Get Today's Move
See what matters today: complete the next task, train steady, recover, or adjust the plan when your readiness and recent load suggest caution.
04
Ask the AI coach
Chat with a coach that uses your recent summaries, goals, workouts, and preferences to explain trends and suggest practical next steps.
05
Track progress
Completed goal tasks are stored as workouts where appropriate, so your plan progress and training history stay connected.
06
Coach linked athletes
Elite users can connect with athletes or trusted contacts using explicit approval and read-only dashboard access.
Integration note: Strava, Garmin, and Samsung are not presented as active direct integrations on this site. Fytvio's primary active pipeline is Apple Health / Google Health Connect, plus manual entries and in-app goal tasks.

Privacy by design

Your health data should work for you.

Fytvio is built for wellness and performance coaching. We do not sell health data, do not use it for advertising, and do not use your coaching conversations to train AI models.

Consent first
Health data and optional health condition flags require explicit consent.
No data selling
No advertisers, data brokers, or cross-app tracking.
Export & delete
Users can export data and request account deletion from the app.
AI transparency
Claude is used for coaching; BYOK users may use their own supported provider key.
