Why Fytvio

Most fitness apps collect data. Fytvio helps you act on it.

Health metrics are useful only when they become decisions. Fytvio analyzes your available data, goals, and training context to help you understand what is changing, why it may matter, and how to respond.

Built around daily training decisions

Fytvio is designed to help you understand readiness, recovery, effort, consistency, and progress without requiring you to interpret every chart yourself.

Context-aware coaching

Your coach can consider recent activity, goals, recovery trends, and available health information before generating guidance.

Adaptive planning

Create fitness goals and receive structured AI-guided plans that evolve as your activity, recovery, and progress change.

For real-life fitness

Use connected health data when available, manual workout entries when needed, and coaching that accounts for incomplete or limited data instead of pretending everything is perfect.

Privacy by design

Your health data should work for you, not be monetized against you.

Fytvio is built around explicit consent, transparent AI use, secure storage, export and deletion rights, and a clear commitment not to sell personal health data.

No data selling

We do not sell, rent, or broker personal health data to advertisers or data brokers.

No AI training

Your health data and coaching conversations are not used to train third-party AI models.

User control

Export data, disconnect sources, and delete your account when needed.

Consent first

Health information is processed with clear consent and privacy safeguards.
