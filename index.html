How it works

From raw data to real insight

Fytvio pulls together your health data from every device, calculates a daily readiness score, and gives you a coach that can explain what it means.

01
Connect your devices
Apple Health, Strava, Garmin, Samsung — connect once and Fytvio syncs automatically in the background.
02
Get your readiness score
Every morning your HRV, sleep quality, resting HR, and training load are combined into a single daily readiness number.
03
Ask your AI coach
Chat with a coach that has full context of your last 30 days of data. Ask why your score dropped, how to structure your week, or whether you should train today.
04
Weekly reviews
Every Sunday get a narrative summary of your week — trends, anomalies, and coaching suggestions delivered as a push notification.
05
Coach linked athletes
Elite plan coaches can connect up to 25 athletes, view their dashboards, and provide personalised coaching — all inside the app.
06
Bring your own AI key
Prefer to use your own Anthropic, OpenAI, or Gemini API key? The BYOK plan gives you full access at a fraction of the cost.

Privacy by design

Your health data is yours

We built Fytvio around the principle that your health data should work for you, not be monetised by us.

✓
No data selling
We never sell, rent, or share your health data with advertisers or data brokers.
✓
No AI training
Your health data and coaching conversations are never used to train any AI model, including Claude.
✓
Export & delete
Download a full copy of your data anytime. Delete your account and all data is permanently erased within 30 days.
✓
Encrypted storage
Data in transit uses TLS 1.2+. Passwords are bcrypt-hashed. BYOK keys never leave your device.
