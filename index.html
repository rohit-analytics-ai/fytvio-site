Why Fytvio

Most fitness apps show you numbers. Fytvio tells you what they mean.

A dashboard full of charts isn't a decision. Fytvio turns your sleep, HRV, steps, and training load into a straight answer — how ready you are today, and what to do about it — plus a coach that can explain the "why" whenever you ask.

One number that actually means something

When sufficient data is available, your readiness score helps summarize the day: push, maintain, or take it easier. Fytvio also explains when available data is limited.

A coach that remembers you

Ask a question and Fytvio can use your current goal, recent sessions, recovery context, and limited conversation history to provide a more relevant answer.

Plans that adjust with you

Set a goal and get a personalized multi-week plan. You can adjust it higher or lower as your schedule, progress, recovery, or goals change.

Built for how training actually goes

Missed a sync, forgot to log a workout, or do not own a smartwatch? Fytvio works with the data you choose to provide and tells you when there is not enough information for confident guidance.

How it works

From "I have no idea how I'm doing" to a clear plan for today.

No setup marathon. Connect what you have, and Fytvio starts making sense of it within your first session.

01

Connect your data

Connect Apple HealthKit or Google Health Connect, or log workouts manually. Fytvio uses only the data sources you authorize.

02

See your readiness

When sufficient recent data is available, Fytvio combines relevant sleep, recovery, and training signals into a readiness score and practical guidance.

03

Set a real goal

A 5K, a strength block, better sleep, just moving more — pick what matters to you and get a plan shaped around it.

04

Ask your coach anything

Ask questions such as “Should I run today?” or “Why does recovery look lower?” Answers are generated from your current Fytvio context and may be imperfect.

05

Watch it add up

Streaks, trends, and completed plans — see the pattern building over weeks, not just today's snapshot.

06

Stay in control

Export available account data, disconnect a source, withdraw consent, or delete your account. Deleting the app alone does not delete your account.

Privacy, for real

You're trusting us with your health data. We don't take that lightly.

Health apps have a bad reputation for quietly monetizing exactly the data you didn't mean to share. Fytvio is built the other way: clear consent, no ad-driven business model, and controls that actually work when you use them.

We never sell your data

Not to advertisers, not to data brokers — full stop, no fine-print exceptions.

Default AI use is not for general model training

We do not authorize our default AI providers to use Fytvio prompts or health data to train their general models. If you use your own API key, your provider account settings and terms also apply.

Real export and deletion

Use available export controls, delete your account in the app, or request deletion through our website. After deletion, only narrowly limited pseudonymous compliance or legal records may remain as described in our Privacy Policy.

Consent you actually gave

Health-data collection and sharing with an AI provider are separate choices. You can withdraw either consent for future processing.
