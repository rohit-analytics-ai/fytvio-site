01

Who we are

Fytvio is operated by ValueSpring LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”). Fytvio is an AI-powered consumer wellness and performance coaching application.

Fytvio is designed to support wellness, fitness, recovery, readiness, and performance coaching. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent disease and should not replace professional medical advice. Fytvio is not a covered entity or business associate under HIPAA.

02

Data we collect

Account information

  • Name and email address
  • Hashed password or identity-provider sign-in information
  • Account creation, login, subscription, and support information

Profile and preferences

  • Optional fitness goals, training focus, units, timezone, height, weight, date of birth, and biological sex where provided
  • Notification preferences, device preferences, coaching preferences, and health consent settings

Health and fitness data

When you connect supported health sources or manually enter information, Fytvio may process metrics such as workouts, steps, distance, calories, sleep, resting heart rate, HRV, heart-rate trends, training load, readiness-related signals, weight, and similar wellness or fitness data where available.

Manual entries

You may manually enter workouts, body measurements, goals, and other fitness information. Manual entries are treated with the same privacy protections as connected health data.

Health conditions and safety context

If you explicitly consent, you may optionally provide health condition flags or injury context from predefined choices. These are used to support safety guardrails in coaching responses. They are not treated as medical diagnoses and are not exposed to linked athletes or coaches.

Usage, logs, and operational data

We collect feature usage, device tokens for push notifications, error logs, security events, subscription status, and operational metadata needed to deliver, secure, and improve the service.

We do not sell, rent, or broker your personal health data to advertisers, data brokers, or third parties for commercial resale.

04

AI coaching

Fytvio uses AI to generate wellness and performance coaching. When you ask for guidance, we may construct a prompt containing your message, recent health and activity summaries, goals, available readiness context, and a limited conversation history.

AI responses may be inaccurate, incomplete, or inappropriate despite safeguards. Use AI coaching as informational support, not as medical advice.

AI providers and model training

Fytvio may use third-party AI providers to generate coaching responses. Your health data and coaching conversations are not used to train third-party AI models through our use of those APIs.

AI operational metadata

We retain limited operational metadata such as model used, timestamps, request identifiers, token usage, latency, and error information to monitor reliability, investigate issues, improve service quality, enforce usage limits, and prevent abuse. This metadata is not used to train third-party AI models.

Bring your own key

If you use the bring-your-own-key feature, your key is stored in encrypted device storage and is not persisted in our database. Your selected provider may receive the coaching prompt needed to generate a response, and that processing may also be subject to your chosen provider's terms and privacy policy.

05

Data quality and validation

Health data can be incomplete, duplicated, delayed, or inconsistent across devices. Fytvio applies validation and anomaly checks to reduce the impact of unrealistic, missing, duplicate, or conflicting measurements before generating insights. Coaching may be limited when available data is insufficient.

06

Linked athletes and coach access

Where available, Fytvio may allow an athlete to grant a coach or trusted contact read-only access to selected aggregated dashboard information. This requires explicit approval and can be revoked. Linked users cannot see raw sensor readings, health condition flags, or private AI coaching conversation history unless a future feature clearly asks for additional consent.

07

08

Data retention

  • Active accounts: We retain data while your account remains active.
  • Deleted accounts: Account deletion begins a grace period where recovery may be possible. After the grace period, personal data is removed from primary systems unless retention is required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes.
  • Backups: Deleted data may remain in encrypted backups until those backups expire or are overwritten.
  • Operational logs: Security, consent, access, and billing-related logs may be retained as needed for legal, compliance, reliability, or abuse-prevention purposes.
09

Your rights and controls

  • Access and export your data where the app supports export.
  • Correct profile information and preferences.
  • Disconnect supported data sources.
  • Withdraw health-data consent where applicable.
  • Delete your account.
  • Contact us to exercise privacy rights available in your jurisdiction.

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under laws such as GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA, or CPRA. We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

10

Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal data, including encrypted connections, password hashing, refresh-token protections, rate limits, access controls, and audit logging. No system is completely secure, but we work to reduce risk and respond responsibly to security issues.

11

Children

Fytvio is not directed to children under 13, or under 16 in the European Economic Area where applicable. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

12

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as Fytvio evolves. Material changes affecting health data, AI use, or data sharing will be communicated by reasonable means such as email, in-app notice, or website notice.
