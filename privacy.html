01

Who we are

Fytvio is operated by ValueSpring LLC ("we", "us", "our"). Fytvio is an AI-powered wellness and performance coaching application for consumers.

Fytvio is for informational, educational, wellness, and performance-coaching purposes only. Fytvio is not medical advice, not a medical device, and not a substitute for a clinician, trainer, emergency service, or professional judgment. Fytvio is not a HIPAA covered entity or business associate.

02

Data we collect

Account and profile data

  • Name, email address, hashed password, account creation date, and last active time.
  • Optional profile context such as goals, training focus, timezone, height, weight, date of birth, biological sex, and unit preference.
  • Authentication and security data such as refresh token hashes, login attempts, and security events.

Health, activity, and workout data

Fytvio reads supported data only after you connect a supported source and grant permission. The current primary active pipelines are Apple Health on iOS and Google Health Connect on Android. Direct Strava, Garmin, and Samsung integrations may be disabled or unavailable depending on provider policy and app configuration.

Optional health condition flags

You may optionally disclose relevant health condition flags, such as asthma, hypertension, diabetes, heart condition, injury, or none of the above. These flags are not treated as diagnoses and are used to add safety guardrails to coaching responses. They are not shown to linked coaches or trusted contacts.

Usage and diagnostic data

We collect feature usage, coaching counts, subscription status, error logs, sync diagnostics, and AI usage metadata so we can operate the service, detect abuse, debug sync issues, and manage plan limits.

03

How we use your data

  • Provide dashboards, readiness scores, trends, insights, and goal task progress.
  • Generate and adjust AI-assisted goal plans.
  • Provide AI coaching based on your recent summaries, goals, workouts, and preferences.
  • Support subscriptions, plan limits, exports, notifications, and account deletion.
  • Improve reliability, prevent abuse, investigate security events, and debug data-quality problems.
We do not sell, rent, or broker your personal data or health data to advertisers or data brokers. We do not use your health data for advertising.
04

AI coaching

Fytvio uses large language models, including Claude by Anthropic, to provide coaching-style explanations and goal guidance. A coaching prompt may include your message, recent health summaries, workout history, goal plan context, profile preferences, timezone, and optional health condition flags.

AI responses may be inaccurate, incomplete, or inappropriate. You are responsible for deciding whether to follow any suggestion. Stop exercising and seek appropriate professional help if you experience concerning symptoms.

Model training

Fytvio does not train AI models on your health data or coaching conversations. API providers may process requests under their own terms and privacy policies for service delivery, abuse prevention, and safety.

BYOK mode

If you use Bring Your Own Key, your key is stored in encrypted device storage where supported. It is sent to Fytvio only when needed to make the requested AI call and is not persisted in our database. Your selected provider's terms also apply.

05

Linked athletes and trusted access

Fytvio may allow an athlete to grant a coach or trusted contact read-only access to selected dashboard data. Both parties must explicitly approve the connection. The athlete can revoke access in the app. Linked users cannot see raw sensor data, health condition flags, private coaching conversation history, or account credentials.

07

Data retention

  • Active accounts: retained while your account is active.
  • Deleted accounts: account deletion starts a deletion flow. Health data, coaching history, and personal data are deleted after the configured grace period unless legally required otherwise.
  • Backups: deleted data may remain in encrypted backups for a limited period until overwritten.
  • Audit logs: consent, security, billing, and linked-access audit records may be retained as needed for compliance, safety, and dispute resolution.
08

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may request access, export, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or withdrawal of consent. You can also disconnect health data sources from your device or app settings. Contact us to exercise privacy rights.

09

Security

  • Data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS.
  • Passwords are hashed; plain-text passwords are not stored.
  • Refresh tokens are stored as hashes and rotated.
  • Plan limits, rate limits, and security event logging help reduce abuse.
  • Optional BYOK keys are not persisted in the Fytvio database.
  • Health condition flags are not exposed through linked-athlete APIs.

No system is completely secure. Please report vulnerabilities responsibly.

10

Children

Fytvio is not directed to children under 13, or under 16 where required by local law. If you believe a child provided data, contact us and we will take appropriate action.
