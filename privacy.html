01

Who we are

Fytvio is operated by ValueSpring LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”). Fytvio is an AI-powered consumer wellness and performance coaching application.

Fytvio is designed to support wellness, fitness, recovery, readiness, and performance coaching. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent disease and should not replace professional medical advice. Fytvio is not a covered entity or business associate under HIPAA. Fytvio is not an emergency service — if you are experiencing a medical or mental health emergency, call 911 (or your local emergency number) immediately.

02

Data we collect

Account information

  • Name and email address
  • Hashed password or identity-provider sign-in information
  • Account creation, login, subscription, and support information

Profile and preferences

  • Optional fitness goals, training focus, units, timezone, height, weight, date of birth, and biological sex where provided
  • Notification preferences, device preferences, coaching preferences, and health consent settings

Health and fitness data

When you connect Apple Health (HealthKit) or Google Health Connect, or manually enter information, Fytvio may process metrics such as workouts, steps, distance, calories, sleep, resting heart rate, HRV, heart-rate trends, training load, readiness-related signals, weight, and similar wellness or fitness data where available.

Location and workout routes

Fytvio does not collect precise GPS location or workout-route data. We also do not use geofencing around health-related locations.

Manual entries

You may manually enter workouts, body measurements, goals, and other fitness information. Manual entries are treated with the same privacy protections as connected health data.

Coach messages and voice input

Messages you send the AI coach—whether typed or created through voice input—are processed to generate coaching responses. Voice input uses your device's operating-system speech-recognition service. Fytvio receives the resulting text and does not intentionally receive or store raw audio. Depending on your device, language, and settings, Apple, Google, or another platform speech service may process the audio under its own terms. You can review or edit the transcript before sending it.

Health conditions and safety context

If you explicitly consent, you may optionally provide health condition flags or injury context from predefined choices. These are used to support safety guardrails in coaching responses. They are not treated as medical diagnoses and are not exposed to linked athletes or coaches.

Usage, logs, and operational data

We collect feature usage, device tokens for push notifications, error logs, security events, subscription status, app version, and operational metadata needed to deliver, secure, support, and improve reliability. Daily-brief notifications may contain health or wellness information, including metrics such as sleep or HRV, depending on the generated brief. Notification previews may appear on your device's lock screen based on your operating-system settings. You can disable notifications or hide previews in Fytvio or your device settings.

We do not sell, rent, or broker your personal health data to advertisers, data brokers, or third parties for commercial resale.

04

AI coaching

Fytvio uses AI to generate wellness and performance coaching. You are interacting with an AI system, not a licensed professional, certified coach, or human being. When you ask for guidance, we may construct a prompt containing your message, recent health and activity summaries, goals, available readiness context, and a limited conversation history.

If you use voice input, your device's operating-system speech-recognition service creates a text transcript. Fytvio receives the transcript and does not intentionally receive or store raw audio. Platform speech providers may process audio under their own privacy terms.

AI responses may be inaccurate, incomplete, or inappropriate despite safeguards. Use AI coaching as informational support, not as medical advice.

Collecting your health data and sharing it with an AI provider to generate coaching are two separate things we ask you to agree to, not one bundled consent. You give each of these separately, on their own terms, before either happens.

We never use your health data to determine employment eligibility or insurance eligibility, and we never use it for unauthorized social sharing.

AI providers and model training

Fytvio uses Anthropic (Claude) as its default AI provider. If you use bring-your-own-key, your selected prompt may instead be sent to OpenAI or Google (Gemini). We do not use AI providers outside these three for coaching. We do not authorize our default AI providers to use Fytvio prompts or health data to train their general models. When you use your own API key, your provider account settings, contractual terms, and privacy policy also apply.

AI operational metadata

We retain limited operational metadata such as model used, timestamps, request identifiers, token usage, latency, and error information to monitor reliability, investigate issues, improve service quality, enforce usage limits, and prevent abuse. This metadata is not used to train third-party AI models.

Bring your own key

Bring-your-own-key is a paid Fytvio platform feature. Your supported Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google API key is stored in encrypted device storage and is not persisted in our database. For each AI request, the key is transmitted securely through Fytvio's server so the server can call the provider, and it is not intentionally logged or retained after request processing. Your selected provider receives the prompt needed to generate a response, and its account settings, terms, privacy policy, usage limits, and separate provider billing also apply.

05

Data quality and validation

Health data can be incomplete, duplicated, delayed, or inconsistent across devices. Fytvio applies validation and anomaly checks to reduce the impact of unrealistic, missing, duplicate, or conflicting measurements before generating insights. Coaching may be limited when available data is insufficient.

06

Consumer health data

Some information Fytvio processes may be “consumer health data” under Washington’s My Health My Data Act and similar state laws. Where those laws apply, this section supplements the rest of this policy.

Categories we may collect

  • Workout, exercise, activity, steps, distance, calories, and training-load information
  • Sleep, resting heart rate, heart-rate trends, HRV, recovery, and readiness-related signals
  • Weight, body measurements, goals, manual entries, and other wellness information you choose to provide
  • Optional health-condition or injury-context flags
  • Inferences such as readiness, recovery, trend, and plan-progress insights

Sources

  • You, including information entered manually
  • Apple HealthKit or Google Health Connect, when authorized
  • Compatible devices that write data into those platforms
  • Fytvio’s own calculations and inferences

Why we collect and use it

  • To provide dashboards, readiness and recovery insights, trends, plans, coaching, safety guardrails, exports, and support
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, troubleshoot synchronization, and improve reliability

Categories we may share

We may share the minimum relevant health summaries, goals, workout context, and prompt context needed to generate AI coaching or operate the service. We do not sell consumer health data.

Recipients

  • Anthropic for default AI coaching
  • OpenAI or Google only when selected through bring-your-own-key
  • Supabase and Render for database, hosting, and application operations
  • Email or push-notification providers only for delivery functions we use
  • Apple and Google for platform services you authorize, such as HealthKit, Health Connect, sign-in, subscriptions, or device speech recognition
  • A linked coach whom you explicitly approve, who can view the health and fitness information displayed in your Today, Trends, and Training dashboard tabs

Consent to collect consumer health data and consent to share it with an AI provider are separate opt-ins. You may withdraw either consent for future processing.

  • We do not use geofencing around locations that could reveal an attempt to seek health services.
  • Access within our systems is limited to what is needed to operate, secure, and support Fytvio.
  • Where applicable, you may request confirmation of whether we collect, share, or sell consumer health data and obtain a list of third parties and affiliates with whom it has been shared.
07

Linked athletes and coach access

Where available, Fytvio allows an athlete to grant a linked coach read-only access to the athlete's full Fytvio Dashboard, including the Today, Trends, and Training tabs. Depending on the data available, this may include readiness, sleep, HRV, heart-rate, steps, activity, workout, training-load, trend, and plan-progress information displayed on those tabs. The athlete must explicitly approve the link and can revoke it. Linked coaches do not receive access to the athlete's Profile screen, account credentials, billing information, health-condition flags, or private AI coaching conversation history unless a future feature clearly requests additional consent.

09

International data transfers

Fytvio operates using servers and service providers located around the world. Depending on which AI provider generates your coaching response — Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google — your prompt may be processed in a different country than the one where you live, and we do not always control or know the specific location of that processing for a given request.

By using Fytvio, you understand that your data may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in countries other than your own, including countries whose data protection laws may differ from those in your own country. We take reasonable steps to require our service providers to protect your information wherever it's processed.

10

Data retention

  • Active accounts: We retain personal, health, coaching, and account data while your account remains active and as needed to provide, secure, and support Fytvio.
  • Deleted accounts: After we verify a deletion request, Fytvio removes personal identifiers, health and activity data, workouts, goals, coaching history, profile data, and other account content from active production systems. Deleting the app alone does not delete your account.
  • Compliance and deletion records: We may retain a limited pseudonymous record needed to demonstrate that consent was obtained or withdrawn and that a deletion request was authenticated and completed. This record may include a one-way keyed hash derived from the account email, consent type and version, relevant timestamps, deletion status, and deletion-process version. It does not retain the email address in readable form and does not include health data, workouts, coaching content, or profile information.
  • Aggregate AI usage metadata: We may retain provider, model, token counts, timestamps, and similar operational metadata that does not contain prompt content, coaching conversations, or health data. On deletion, these records are reassigned to a shared placeholder so they cannot be isolated as a deleted user's usage history.
  • Billing, fraud, and legal records: We may retain limited subscription, transaction, chargeback, fraud-prevention, security, or legal records when reasonably necessary to comply with law, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, or protect the service. We minimize identifying information and do not retain health data in these records.
  • Backups: Encrypted backup copies may persist after deletion according to our infrastructure provider's backup rotation. Where Washington's My Health My Data Act applies, consumer health data in archived or backup systems will be deleted no later than six months after we authenticate the deletion request.

Your rights and controls

  • Access and export your data where the app supports export.
  • Correct profile information and preferences.
  • Disconnect supported data sources.
  • Withdraw health-data consent where applicable.
  • Delete your account.
  • Contact us to exercise privacy rights available in your jurisdiction.
  • Where applicable, request confirmation of whether we collect, share, or sell consumer health data and obtain a list of third parties and affiliates with whom it has been shared.

Account-deletion instructions are available at fytvio.com/delete-account.html.

Deleting your Fytvio account removes the Fytvio account and covered data. If you used Sign in with Apple or Google Sign-In, authorization with that identity provider is separate. Fytvio will follow the applicable provider revocation process where supported; you may also manage connected-app access in your Apple or Google account.

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under laws such as GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA, or CPRA. We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

To exercise any of these rights, email info@valuespringllc.com. We will verify your request and respond within 45 days, or sooner where required by law; if we need more time, we'll tell you why.

If we deny your request, we'll explain why in writing. You can appeal that decision by replying to our response within 30 days; we'll respond to your appeal within 45 days. If we deny the appeal, we'll provide instructions for contacting the Washington State Attorney General (or the relevant regulator in your jurisdiction) to submit a complaint.

After account deletion, Fytvio may retain only the limited pseudonymous compliance, deletion, billing, security, or legal records described in the retention section. These records are not used to recreate your account, provide coaching, or restore deleted health data.

12

Security and breach notification

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal data, including encrypted connections, password hashing, refresh-token protections, access controls, rate limits, session-version checks, and audit logging. A password change or reset invalidates previously issued sessions according to our security controls. No system is completely secure.

If we discover a breach affecting your personal data, we will notify affected users and, where required, relevant regulators without undue delay and in line with applicable law, including the FTC's Health Breach Notification Rule, which applies to health apps like Fytvio that are not covered by HIPAA.

13

Age requirement

Fytvio is intended only for adults 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly allow anyone under 18 to create or use an account or knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. Users must confirm they are at least 18 before account creation and before health-data or AI-sharing consent is accepted.

If we learn that an account belongs to someone under 18, we will suspend access and delete the account and associated personal data after appropriate verification, subject to the retention practices described above.
