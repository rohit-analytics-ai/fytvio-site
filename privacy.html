04

AI coaching and Anthropic

Fytvio's coaching feature is powered by Claude, a large language model developed by Anthropic, PBC. When you send a coaching message, we construct a prompt that includes:

  • Your message text
  • A summary of your recent health metrics (heart rate, sleep, training load, readiness score)
  • Your profile context (goals, health condition flags if disclosed, timezone)
  • A short window of recent conversation history (Pro and Elite plans)

This prompt is sent to Anthropic's API over an encrypted connection. Anthropic processes the data to generate a response, which is returned to you in real time. AI-generated responses may occasionally be inaccurate, incomplete, or inappropriate despite the safety controls implemented within the platform.

Anthropic's data use

Under Anthropic's API terms of service, Anthropic does not train its models on API customers' data. Your health data and coaching conversations are not used to train Claude or any Anthropic model. Anthropic may retain API request data for a limited period for safety and abuse-prevention purposes, subject to their own privacy policy.

Bring your own key (BYOK)

If you use the BYOK feature, your API key is stored exclusively in encrypted on-device storage (iOS Keychain / Android EncryptedSharedPreferences). Your key is transmitted to our server only to make the AI API call on your behalf and is never persisted in our database. In BYOK mode, your coaching data is also governed by your chosen provider's privacy terms.

ⓘ

You can withdraw consent for health data processing at any time by deleting your account. This permanently removes all health data from our systems after a 30-day grace period.

05

Linked athletes (coach access)

Fytvio allows an athlete (person A) to grant a coach or trusted contact (person B) read-only access to their health dashboard. This feature requires explicit action from both parties and the following protections apply:

  • Both parties must explicitly approve the connection through the Fytvio application before access is granted.
  • Person A is notified when access is requested or activated, and can revoke access at any time in-app.
  • Person B can only view aggregated dashboard data — they cannot see raw sensor readings, health condition flags, or coaching conversation history.
  • Health condition flags are never exposed through the linked-athlete API. Coaching guardrails derived from those conditions are applied server-side without revealing the underlying conditions.
  • All data access by Person B is logged to an access audit trail.
  • Person A can revoke access at any time from Profile → Athletes.

If a coach's subscription is downgraded, all linked-athlete access is automatically revoked and affected athletes are notified by email.

Data retention

  • Active accounts: We retain your data for as long as your account is active.
  • Deleted accounts: When you delete your account, it enters a 30-day grace period during which you may reverse the deletion. After 30 days, all health data, coaching history, and personal information are permanently deleted from our primary database. Anonymised aggregate statistics derived from your data (e.g. cohort averages used for readiness benchmarks) may be retained but cannot be used to re-identify you.
  • Backup retention: Database backups may retain deleted data for up to 30 additional days before those backups are overwritten.
  • Consent records: Our consent audit log retains a record of when consent was given and withdrawn, as required by GDPR. This record contains no health data — only timestamps and consent version identifiers.
  • Access audit log: Linked-athlete access events are retained for 12 months.

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Security

We take the security of health data seriously and implement the following technical measures:

  • All data is transmitted over TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with a per-user salt — plain-text passwords are never stored.
  • Refresh tokens are stored as SHA-256 hashes — a database leak cannot be replayed to gain access.
  • Access tokens have a 2-hour lifetime; refresh tokens expire after 30 days and are rotated on every use.
  • Login attempts are rate-limited at the network level and at the account level — accounts lock after 10 failed attempts for 15 minutes.
  • Health condition flags are never returned through linked-athlete API endpoints.
  • BYOK API keys are stored only in encrypted device storage (iOS Keychain / Android EncryptedSharedPreferences) and are not persisted on our servers.

Despite these measures, no system is completely secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to info@valuespringllc.com.
